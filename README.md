# Mosaic PRD

Mosaic transforms collaboration into attributable experience.

## Tagline

Every role is a piece.

## Problem

Students are often expected to build entire projects independently despite real-world products requiring multiple specialized roles. This results in shallow experience and weak portfolios.

## Solution

Mosaic enables aspiring professionals to collaborate through their domain specialization while receiving attributable credit for their contributions.

## Core Positioning

Instead of asking:

"Do you have experience?"

Mosaic asks:

"What did you build, and what was your contribution?"
